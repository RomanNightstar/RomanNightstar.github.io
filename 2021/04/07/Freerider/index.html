Freerider? Freerider!

2021-04-07

What creates your nightmare – A Despicable Freerider!

Let’s recall some old university days. Imagine that one day a friend comes by and proposes to take one course togother that contains a group project.

Well, you get stuck and start to hesitate, bacause you know this person is anything but smart. She has a low GPA, and does not work hard, at least from your perspective, and is rather stupid and testy sometimes. If possible, you really would like to avoid any teamwork with her. But you’ve been friends (you can tolerate her numbness in daily life if that does not cause you trouble) and she swears that although she is not as intelligent nor knowledgable, she will definitely work hard on. Well, you think, although that project is challenging, and requires much intellectual power, probably there are still miscellaneous tasks you don’t wish to waste your energy on. You see this person begging you sincerely (at least from the appearance) and saying that she cannot make it by herself and she would definitely fail without you, so you agree out of mercy. You know you are gonna have a slightly harder time on this project, but based on your estimation you believe it would be fine.

But it is NOT! That is how your nightmare could start with this freerider!

Highly probably this person is by NO means NOT a freerider, whose alleged friendship is anything but fake. You are simply a knucklehead she can exploit for her easy life, and she doesn’t give a shit about your life or death, evident from the fact that you can recollect from memory that she only mentioned how miserable she would be if there was no such a considerate kucklehead to exploit.

What is a typical freerider?

In my personal specification, most of the freeriders in this world can be classified into four categories: Innocent Freeriders, Overt Freeriders, Chameleon Freeriders and Star-lord Freeriders. Contradictory to common sense, among the three probably Overt Freeriders are the least harmful, while Chameleon Freeriders are the most.

I would like to give below my definitions of these four categories of freeriders. The four are not strictly mutually exclusive, and one freerider display characterictics typical of multiple categories.

What is an innocent freerider?

Innocent Freerider

An Innocent Freerider, as the name suggests, is innocent in some way. Physically and interllectually they are competent, even superior over you in some qualities. This kind of freeriders don’t really mean to freeride on you, but certain constraints force them to take the freerider role. For example, it could be that they are simultaneously taking some extremely important and challenging tasks that consume all their time and energy so that they are unable to join you, although they sincerely hope they can. It could also be that they themselves face freeriders as well in other tasks. They would express their appreciation for you, try to compensate by taking more responsibilities once they are emancipated from other undertakings, and treat you to a dinner as personal apology.

Although their behaviour observed from your stance is absolute freeriding, by all means this first category is innocent, because they don’t intentionally exploit you like greedy capitalists, but rather they are victims as well in some sense. If I were a judge to indict freeriders in court, then I would definitely announce this category guilty, but at most misdemeanor.
